Ingredients

  • Canned Tuna
  • Mayonnaise
  • Dill
  • Salt
  • Lemon Juice
  • Celery
  • Red Onion

Steps

  1. Drain tuna well and transfer to a small bowl. Using a fork, flake tuna apart into strands.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk mayonnaise, dill, salt, pepper, and 1 tablespoon lemon juice. Stir in pickles, celery, and onion, then fold in tuna. Taste and add remaining 1 tablespoon lemon juice as desired.
  3. Serve on bread or lettuce (if using).
