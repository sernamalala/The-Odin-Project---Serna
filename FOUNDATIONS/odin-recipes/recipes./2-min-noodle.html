Description

Noodle dinners don't get much easier than this. Using 2-minute noodles, French onion soup mix, and cheese, we've created the ultimate lazy dinner. It's perfect for those nights when you're just not bothered!

Ingredients

  • 1 packet 2-minute chicken noodles
  • 1 tbsp French onion soup mix
  • 10g unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Remove noodles from packet and reserve flavour sachet for another use (see notes). Cook noodles in a saucepan with 350ml boiling water for 2 minutes or until noodles are tender.
  2. Discard liquid as desired (see notes). Add French onion soup mix to noodles and stir until well combined.
  3. Place noodles in a serving bowl and stir in butter and cheese. Serve.
